## 🖌 Overview  
Audio Hijack Advanced is a flexible audio capture and recording application that lets you record audio from any application, device, or system source using a visual block-based routing system that makes complex audio setups intuitive to build and manage.

The session editor represents your audio flow as a connected graph of input, processing, and output blocks. Drag in a source, add processing effects, connect to a recorder, and your routing is complete without any manual configuration of system audio settings.

Capture audio from browsers, video conferencing apps, streaming services, system sounds, and microphones — individually or simultaneously — routing each source through its own processing chain.

Built-in effects blocks include EQ, compressor, noise reduction, and more, letting you process your captured audio in real time without requiring a separate audio editor after recording.

For podcasters, streamers, musicians, and anyone who needs to record audio from sources that do not normally support direct capture, Audio Hijack Advanced is the routing and recording tool that makes it possible.
